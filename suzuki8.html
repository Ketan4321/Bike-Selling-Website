Suzuki has given its popular Gixxer naked bike some premium treatment in the form of the new Gixxer 250. Boasting a significant bump in displacement and the addition of some premium features, the Suzuki Gixxer 250 carries over the commuter bike's well-loved performance and utility, all at a price tag of just P165,000.

The Suzuki Gixxer 250 features a fully digital instrument panel which offers an abundance of information. It also comes with an ergonomic split-type seat which ensures maximum comfort for both rider and passenger, especially on longer rides.

At the heart of the Gixxer 250 is a 249cc oil-cooled, four-stroke-single cylinder engine. Equipped with a single-overhead-cam, this engine pumps out a decent 26 hp and 22 Nm of torque. Equipped with Suzuki's latest fuel-injection technology, this engine not only provides adequate performance, but impressive fuel efficiency, as well.

Disc brakes on both wheels are present in this sport bike to ensure stopping prowess on both road and track for the safety of the rider.
