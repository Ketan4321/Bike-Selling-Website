The Suzuki Skydrive Sport variant is the more fashionable choice, while the 125 Fi is a more dependable ride. The Sport variant is priced at ₹67,900 and the 125 Fi is tagged at ₹78,900.

Key features include a key shutter and a dual front rack. The 125 Fi has a headlight with five lights for visual impact, while the Sport has styling cues from the GSX-R bigger sibling.

The 125 Fi is powered by a 124cc, air-cooled, fuel-injected engine, with 9 hp and 10 Nm of torque. The Sport uses an 113cc engine with 9 hp and 9 Nm of torque, both variants feature CVT transmission.

Disc brakes in the front and drums at the rear, along with key shutter security, ensure smooth performance and safety.
